Work with CanSat teams

CanSat is an international engineering team competition whose premise is to send a can-sized satellite 3000 metres into the atmosphere. A large part of the project is marketing in order to get sponsors to fund a project. Over the last two years, I have helped a few CanSat teams with their websites and video ads which included both 2D and 3D animation. Moreover, I conducted a workshop on how to do such things.

Samsung Solve For Tomorrow

I was a member of a team that took part in the Samsung Solve for Tomorrow competition. Our project aimed to help people with shortcomings often connected with health. We created an app called Pocket Doctor which reminded the user to take medication, taught them how to lead a healthier and longer life and contained a special medical card which could be accessed in life-threatening situation. I was the lead designer of the app and largely helped code it.
